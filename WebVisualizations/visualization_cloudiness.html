Cloudiness

There is no clear correlation between latitude and cloudiness. At most latitudes, you can find cities with widely varying cloudiness. This means that other factors like humidity and elevation might more important than latitude in determining the cloudiness of a place. What is interesting is that there aren't too many distinct cloudiness values. Instead there are relatively few and varying values, but a lot of cities with each value.
